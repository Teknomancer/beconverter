Available categories

The BeConverter, by default, comes with the following categories of data equivalents. Each category has several units which you can convert to and from. The units inside each category are not listed in the documentation, since, there need not be any explanations on a unit. Here only the category names are listed.

Acceleration Angle Angular Acceleration
Angular Velocity Area Capacitance
Charge Coverage Current
Density Digital Quantity Distance
Energy Force Fuel Consumption
Illuminance Inductance Luminance
Magnetic Flux Mass Money
Power Pressure Specific Heat
Temperature Thermal Conductivity Through-put
Time Torque Velocity
Viscosity Voltage Volume

Apart from the above categories you can create and use your own categories in BeConverter. For detailed information read the "Custom categories" section of this manual.

BeConverter convert just about anything... and having so many default categories you can virtually convert to and from any standard as well as scientific quantities. What makes BeConverter versatile and useful is the collection of all these categories.
